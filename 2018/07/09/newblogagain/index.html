Newblogagain

9 Jul, 2018 · Read in about 1 min · (186 words) · Share this on:
Blog

Once again I’m making a new blog. The previous ‘New blog’ blog was about two years ago.

In the middle many adventures, ideas, books, conferences, code, gadgets and other stuff of my interest happened.

Now, new blog, using the same static generator written in Go as before, to create it ( Hugo ), with new theme and some ideas.

I hope in this brand new ‘New blog’ I could reach what I would like to complete now.

If you see the since year in the copyright you are gonna find 2006, because that is the year of the foundation of my blog. I keep that date, even when no post is older than 2 years is present, because I might want to add old stuff and because I like that year. So, now you know the why of that :-)

A final, and not important, note: this brand new blog has an amazing word-counter and estimated-time-reading that you can find in the header of each post. I do like these features a lot!

Until the next post ( time window, hopefully lesser than two years )!
